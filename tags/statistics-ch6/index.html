Variance of continuous random variable

Definition If $X$ is a continuous random variable with $E(X) = \mu$, then $Var(X)$ and $\sigma_X$, called the variance and standard deviation of $X$, respectively, are defined by $$ Var(X) = E[(X-\mu)^2] = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} (x-\mu)^2 f(x) dx \\ \sigma_X = \sqrt{E[(X-\mu)^2]} $$
